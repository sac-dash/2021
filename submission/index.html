Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original papers: they must not substantially duplicate work that any of the authors have published elsewhere or have submitted in parallel to journals or any other conferences that have proceedings.

Submissions must be formatted using two-columns and may be up to 6 pages in length, including all tables, figures, appendices, and references. We also welcome 2 page short paper submissions.

Download the paper form below, and submit the paper by July 31th through the Easychair paper submission site. ACM format style LaTex and MS-Word templates for SMA 2020 are available here:

  • MS-Word Templates: [MS-Word]
